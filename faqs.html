Reason for initiating this portal?

The centralized Greivance Redressal is to collect the greivances of the students within a resonable time period for further strengthening the bond of students with the institution by providing them with all kind of facilities to a satisfaction level.

It aims to look into the complaints lodged by any student and redress it as per requirement. The institution aims at solving the greivances of the students within stipulated time.
